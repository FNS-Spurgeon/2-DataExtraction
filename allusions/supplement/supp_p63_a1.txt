1877. Selby, Walford D. Two Chaucer Documents [in] The Academy.
Oct. 13, 1877, pp. 364-5.
[The discovery by Floyd of the conveyances passing the
quit-rent of the tenement in St. Botolph Without Aldgate, by
John and Agnes Chaucer, and Bartholomew atte Chapel and
Agnes, to Nicholas Long, and releasing to him all right of
dower; with the text of the documents. The chief points
deduced are—
1. The date of John Chaucer's death, to within a year.
2. The re-marriage of Agnes Chaucer.
3. A Chaucer seal different from any seal hitherto found.
Ou the documents see A. A. Kern: The Ancestry of Chaucer,
pp. 95, 97.]

1876. Haweis, Mary Eliza. Chaucer for Children, A Golden Key,
illustrated with eight coloured Pictures, and numerous Woodcuts
by the Author, 1877.
[With a preface "To the Mother" on the reading and
pronunciation of Chaucer, followed by a biographical sketch
of Chaucer. Abridged stories from "The Canterbury Tales"
and a few Minor Poems are then given.]

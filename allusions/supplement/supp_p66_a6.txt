1878. Storr, Francis (the Younger), and Turner, Hawes, Canterbury
Chimes, or Chaucer Tales retold for Children, 1878.
[A very free rendering, in simple modern English, of the
Prologue to the C. Tales, and an abridgment of the Knight's,
Man of Law's, Nonne Preestes, Squire’s, Franklin's, and
"Chaucer's Tale" (Gamelyn, which is purposely inserted
instead of Sir Thopas, as being more suitable, see Preface, p.
vi), illustrated by woodcuts from the Ellesmere MS.]

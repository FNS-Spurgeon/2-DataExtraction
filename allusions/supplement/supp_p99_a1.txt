1884. Ruskin, John. A Lecture on Patience delivered at Oxford,
November 22, 1884, and reported by E. T. Cook in the Pall Mall
Gazette of November 21, 1884. (Reprinted in Cook’s Studies in
Ruskin, 1890.) Works of Ruskin, Library edn., ed. E. T. Cook and
A. Wedderburn, 1908, vol. xxxiii, p. 525.
[The report reads:]
The first passage read on Saturday was the analysis of
Chaucer’s "Patience":—
"Dame Patiencë sitting there I fonde,
With facë pale, upon an hill of sonde. . . ."

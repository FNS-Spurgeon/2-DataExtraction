1871. Furnivall, F. J. Balade by Chaucer, [a leaf, reprinted from] The
Athenæum, No. 2260, London, 1871, p. 210.
[A balade from Addit. MS. 16, 158, 2 stanzas of 7,
beginning:]
Sith hit is so / þer as hit may not freese
þat cuery night / but I haþe sume solas
I wol me venge on loue as doþe a breese [etc.]
This turned out to be only the second stanza of the balade.
The whole was printed by Dr. Furnivall with Jyl of Breynt¬
ford, 1871: see below.

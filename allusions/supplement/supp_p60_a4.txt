1877. Green, John Richard. Letter to Dr. F. J. Furnivall, dated
Richmond House, Boughton, Chester, March 12, 1877. (In the
possession of Mr. Percy Furnivall.)
I am as hungry as ever for your 'Chaucer.' Do let me
have it.
[The book on Chaucer referred to was to form one of
the series of primers brought out by Green, the Chaucer
one was eventually written by A. W. Pollard. See Letters
of J. R. Green, edited by Leslie Stephen, 1901, p. 449.]

1870. Purves, D. Laing. The Canterbury Tales and Fairie Queene:
with Other Poems of Chaucer and Spenser. Edited for Popular
Perusal (Edinburgh, 1870).
[Preceded by a Preface, mentioning that the object of the
volume is to place before the general reader the Canterbury
Tales and Fairie Queene in a way that will render their
"popular perusal" easy. Followed by a Life of Chaucer and
some remarks on his poems.]

1881. Bloxam, W. M. On Chaucer's Monument in Westminster Abbey
[in] Archæological Journal, vol. xxxviii, 361-4.
[Showing that though the tomb was put up by Nicholas
Brigham in 1555, its date is about 1470-80, and it was probably
bought for about 6s. 8d. when the tombs of the Grey Friars
and other Churches were pulled down in 1547-52, and then
set up by Brigham to the memory of Chaucer.]

1399, Oct. 13. Grant by Henry IV. to Chaucer, for good service rendered
to the new King, of an annuity of 40 marks, in addition to the £20
given him by Richard II. Patent Roll, 1 Hen. IV., p. 5, m. 12
(Kirk, 327. Godwin, App. xxvi).

1398, May 20. Action of Debt in the Common Pleas by Isabella, widow
and administratrix of Walter Bukholt, Esquire, against Geoffrey
Chaucer, Esquire, for £14 1s. 11d.; and against John Goodale of
Milleford, for £12 8s. The Sheriff of Middlesex returns that they
have nothing [in his bailwick], and he is ordered to arrest them
De Banco Roll, Easter, 21 Ric. Il., m. 368 d (Kirk, 321, and note
1, 322; see also the Athenœum, Sept. 13, 1879, p. 338).

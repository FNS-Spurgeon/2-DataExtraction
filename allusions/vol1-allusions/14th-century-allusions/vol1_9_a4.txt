" July 14 and 22. Two payments to Chaucer, as Clerk of the
Works, for expenses at Westminster, the Tower, and elsewhere.
Issue Roll, Easter, 12 Ric. Il., m. 13.
[Chaucer held the office of Clerk of the Works from July 12, 1389, to June 17,
1391. These payments continue at intervals, 25 in all, on the following dates—
Oct 7, Nov. 23, Dec. 1, 14, 24, 1389; Mar. 3, 4, June 4, 16, 17, 25, July 9, 19, Oct.
28, Dec. 6, 7, 1390; Feb. 28, Mar. 20, April 20, Dec. 16, 1391; Mar. 4. July 18, 1392,
on which last-named date a final payment of arrears due as Clerk ot the Works was
made to Chancer by the King.] (Kirk, 278-80, 286-7, 289-90, 297, 314-5, also Introd.
pp. xxxvi-xxix, xlii-xliv.)

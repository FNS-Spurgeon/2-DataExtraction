[1596-7.] Shakespeare, William. The First Part of King Henry IV,
III, iii, 57.
How now, Dame Partlet the hen!
[The allusion must clearly be to Chaucer’s Nonne Preestes Tale, as he first gave the
name of 'Pertelote' to the hen. In the Roman de Renart and Reinhart Fuchs, the
hen’s name is 'Pinte.'  See also below, App. A, 1589, Shakespeare.]

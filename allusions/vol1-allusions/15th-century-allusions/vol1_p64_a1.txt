And sene I haue spokin samekle of this noble and haly
virgin I will in the end of þis buk writ ane orisoune þat
Galfryde Chauceir maid and prayit to þis lady And thoᵗ I be
noᵗ Eloquent in þis tovng as was þat noble poet I wil writ her
twa orisouns in lattin that I maid of þis noble and excellent
lady and send furth of parice with a buk that I maid of hir
concepcioun to þi fader of gud mynd The first is of þe gret
honoᴿ and dignite of þis lady And the second is of hir noble
and haly byrth of hir blist son Ihsus.

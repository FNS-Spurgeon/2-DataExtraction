1421-2. Hoccleve, Thomas. [Dialogus cum Amico.] MS. Durham iii,
9, fol. 23 b. (Hoccleves works, ed. F. J. Furnivall, E. E. T. soc.
vol i, The minor poems, 1892, p. 135, Il. 694-7.)
The wyf of Bathe, take I for auctrice
þat wommen han no ioie ne deyntee
þat men sholde vp-on hem putte any vice;
I woot wel so / or Iyk to þat, seith shee.
CHAUCER CRITICISM.

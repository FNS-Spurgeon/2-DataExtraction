1471. Bruyn, Elizabeth. Will of Dame Elizabeth Brune in South
Ockendon church, Essex (Excerpts from ancient wills, by H. W.
King; transactions Essex archæol. soc., 1884, new series, vol. ii, pp.

I will that Robert Walsall have the boke called Canterbury
tales, and one gilt cup wᵗ ye coueryng, and one sparuer of
silke, and a diall of gold, and ij hors in my stable, and j double
harpe.

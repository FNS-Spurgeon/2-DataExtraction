[c. 1420] Unknown. Headline to Sir Thopas's end link, in MSS.
Ellesmere (fol. 157) and Hengwrt (fol, 215). (Six-text Canterbury
Tales, ed. F. J. Furnivall, Chaucer soc., 1871-8, p. 199, parts i-iii.)
[Ellesmere] ¶ Heere the Host stynteth Chaucer / of his tale
of Thopas.
[Hengwrt] ¶ Here the hoost / stynteth Chaucer of his tale
of Thopas / and biddeth hym / telle another tale.

[c. 1450.] Shirley, John (?). Headline to The Compleynt of Mars. MS.
Trin. Coll. Camb. R. 3. 20, fol. 130. (Parallel-text of Chaucer's
minor poems, ed. F. J. Furnivall, Chaucer soc. [1871], p. 100.)
¶ Loo yee louers gladeþe and comforteþe you . of þallyance
etrayted bytwene / þe hardy and furyous Mars . þe god of
armes / and Venus þe double goddesse of loue made by
.Geffrey Chaucier. at þe comandement of þe renomed and
excellent Prynce my lord þe Duc Iohn of Lancastre.

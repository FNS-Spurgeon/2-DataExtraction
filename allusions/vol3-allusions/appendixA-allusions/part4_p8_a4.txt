[c. 1500.] Unknown. A ryght pleasaunt and merye Historie of the
Mylner of Abyngdon, with his vife, and his fayre daughter: and
of two poor scholers of Cambridge... Imprinted at London by
Rycharde Ihones.
[The unique copy of this edition is in the Bodleian; that
of Wynkyn de Worde’s, also undated, was at Britwell. The
poem is probably much older than any printed edition.
The plot is that of the Reves Tale, but it may be inde¬
pendently derived from a French fabliau. Ses the reprint
in Thomas Wright’s Anecdota Literaria, 1844.]

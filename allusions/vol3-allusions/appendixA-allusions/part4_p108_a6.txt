1708. Unknown. Inscription on the tomb of John Phillips, in West-
minster Abbey, next to Chaucer's.
. . . . .
Auso licèt a Tuâ Metrorum Lege discedere,
Fas sit Huic
O Poesis Anglicanæ Pater atque Conditor, CHAUCERE,
Vatum certé Cineres Tuos undique stipantium
Alterum Tibi latus * claudere.
* i.e. the south side, the north being occupied by Cowley.
Non dedecebit Chorum.

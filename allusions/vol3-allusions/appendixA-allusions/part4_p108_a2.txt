[n.a. 1603.] Elizabeth, Queen. Quotation of Reves Tale, l. 134.
“ The grettest Clerkes been noght the wysest men."
[Sir Walter Scott reports Queen Elizabeth's quotation of
this line to the Bishop of St. David’s in Tales of a Grandfather
(q.v. above, 1829), and alludes to it in The Monastery and in
Kenilworth (qq.v. above, 1820 and 1821). We have not
been able to identify the occasion or to verify the story,
which may be of Scott’s invention.]

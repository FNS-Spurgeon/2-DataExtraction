Sermon [quoted by John Manningham in his Diary for 1602, (ed.
J. Bruce, Camden Soc., 1868, p. 11).]
All our new corne comes out of old feilds, and all our
[Rollins 52.]
new learning is gathered out of old bookes. (Chaucer.)


82 Five Hundred Years of [A.D. 1536-
For Iyke the mone euer waxe ye and wane:
Your reason halteth, your jugement is lawe,
Your dome is false, your constance euyll preueth,
A full great foole is he that on you leneth.
[An imperfect rendering of Clerkes Tale, ll. 994-1001.]
